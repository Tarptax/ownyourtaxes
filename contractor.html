Contractors Edition

The Contractors Edition is designed for independent contractors, freelancers, consultants, and self-employed professionals who receive income outside of traditional employment.

This guide focuses on practical tax education, recordkeeping, estimated tax payments, and strategies that can help contractors stay organized throughout the year.
