Basic Business Tax Bootcamp

 

Seminar Workbook & Action Guide

 

The Basic Business Tax Bootcamp is an interactive workshop workbook designed to help business owners better understand taxes, bookkeeping, profit, cash flow, tax planning, and financial organization.

 

Unlike a traditional tax book, this workbook includes exercises, self-assessments, worksheets, planning tools, note-taking pages, and a guided 90-day action plan that helps participants apply what they learn to their own businesses.

Throughout the workshop, participants build a practical framework for managing taxes and finances:

 
 

1. Track Income

2. Track Expenses

3. Know Your Profit

4. Save For Taxes

5. Review Monthly

6. Plan Ahead

7. Stay Consistent

 
 
 

Designed For Action

 

Many business owners collect information but never implement it. The purpose of this workbook is to bridge the gap between learning and action.

 

Each module encourages participants to evaluate their current systems, identify weaknesses, create improvement plans, and begin building habits that can reduce tax stress and improve financial awareness throughout the year. 【1-eb9497】

 
 

90-Day Tax Action Plan

 

The workbook concludes with a structured 90-day action plan that helps participants develop bookkeeping habits, establish tax savings systems, improve organization, and gain greater confidence in understanding their numbers.
