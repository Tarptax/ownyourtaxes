Shipping & Packaging Expenses

For many online sellers, shipping and packaging are essential parts of doing business. Whether you sell on Etsy, eBay, Amazon, your own website, or through social media platforms, maintaining organized records related to shipping and packaging can help improve business organization and provide a clearer picture of overall operating costs.

Many sellers focus heavily on product sales while overlooking the expenses associated with getting products into customers' hands. Developing a system for tracking shipping and packaging activity can help support better financial management throughout the year.

Why Shipping & Packaging Records Matter

Shipping-related costs can accumulate quickly, particularly for sellers who fulfill orders regularly.

Maintaining organized records can help:

  • Monitor business expenses.
  • Understand product profitability.
  • Improve inventory planning.
  • Support financial organization.
  • Prepare for tax season more efficiently.

Tracking these costs consistently often provides a more accurate picture of business operations.

Common Shipping Expenses

Many online sellers incur shipping-related costs on a regular basis.

Examples may include:

  • Postage purchases
  • Shipping label costs
  • Carrier service fees
  • Package tracking fees
  • Delivery-related charges

Maintaining organized records of these expenses can simplify future reviews.

Common Packaging Expenses

Packaging materials often represent a significant operating cost for online businesses.

Examples may include:

  • Shipping boxes
  • Padded mailers
  • Bubble wrap
  • Packing paper
  • Tape
  • Shipping labels
  • Protective inserts
  • Branded packaging materials

Even small packaging purchases can add up over time, making regular tracking important.

Track Costs by Product Line

Many sellers find it useful to review shipping and packaging costs by product category.

This can help answer questions such as:

  • Which products cost the most to ship?
  • Which products require the most packaging materials?
  • Which products generate the strongest margins?

Breaking costs into categories can provide valuable business insights.

Save Shipping Documentation

Shipping records can provide useful support for financial organization.

Consider maintaining:

  • Shipping receipts
  • Carrier invoices
  • Postage purchase records
  • Shipping platform summaries
  • Order fulfillment reports

Digital storage often makes these records easier to organize and retrieve.

Maintain Packaging Supply Records

Many sellers reorder packaging materials throughout the year.

Keeping records of packaging purchases can help:

  • Monitor inventory levels.
  • Estimate future needs.
  • Avoid supply shortages.
  • Improve purchasing decisions.

Tracking packaging inventory can be just as important as tracking product inventory.

Create Shipping & Packaging Categories

Many business owners benefit from organizing records into specific categories.

Examples may include:

  • Postage
  • Boxes & Mailers
  • Protective Packaging
  • Labels
  • Branded Packaging
  • Shipping Software
  • Carrier Fees

Consistent categories can simplify reviews and reporting.

Review Costs Monthly

A monthly review can help sellers better understand operational costs and identify trends.

  • □ Review postage expenses.
  • □ Review packaging purchases.
  • □ Monitor inventory levels.
  • □ Organize shipping receipts.
  • □ Save carrier reports.
  • □ Update inventory records.
  • □ Back up important documents.

Regular reviews are often easier than year-end organization projects.

Common Shipping & Packaging Mistakes

Ignoring Small Purchases

Small purchases such as tape, labels, and packaging materials can add up significantly over time.

Not Saving Shipping Records

Shipping documentation can help support a more complete recordkeeping system.

Failing to Track Packaging Inventory

Running out of packaging supplies can disrupt business operations and delay order fulfillment.

Reviewing Costs Only at Tax Time

Regular reviews provide better visibility into business performance throughout the year.

Shipping & Packaging Checklist

  • □ Save shipping receipts.
  • □ Track postage purchases.
  • □ Record packaging expenses.
  • □ Monitor packaging inventory.
  • □ Organize carrier invoices.
  • □ Create expense categories.
  • □ Review costs monthly.
  • □ Back up important records.

The Bottom Line

Shipping and packaging expenses are important parts of many ecommerce and online-selling businesses. Understanding these costs, organizing records, and reviewing expenses regularly can help sellers improve business organization and make more informed decisions.

The goal is not simply shipping products—it is creating a system that helps you understand your costs, maintain organized records, and operate a more efficient business throughout the year.
