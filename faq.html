Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common tax questions for small business owners, contractors, musicians, online sellers, and gig workers.

General Tax Questions

Why is tax education important?

Understanding taxes helps individuals and business owners make informed financial decisions, avoid common mistakes, and stay organized throughout the year.

What records should I keep for tax purposes?

Keep records of income, expenses, receipts, bank statements, invoices, mileage logs, and any tax documents you receive throughout the year.

How long should I keep tax records?

Many taxpayers keep important records for several years after filing a return. Record retention needs can vary depending on individual circumstances.

Small Business Owners

What expenses can a small business deduct?

Common business expenses may include office supplies, software, advertising, insurance, professional services, equipment, and other ordinary and necessary business costs.

Do I need a separate bank account for my business?

While requirements vary, keeping business and personal finances separate often makes bookkeeping and tax preparation much easier.

What tax records should a business owner track?

Business owners should track income, expenses, receipts, invoices, mileage, payroll records, and other documents related to business activity.

Independent Contractors

Do contractors pay taxes differently than employees?

Independent contractors are generally responsible for tracking income, paying their own taxes, and maintaining records of business expenses.

Can contractors deduct mileage?

Vehicle expenses related to business activities may qualify for tax deductions depending on how the vehicle is used and documented.

Why is expense tracking important?

Proper expense tracking helps contractors understand profitability and maintain accurate records throughout the year.

Gig Workers

Do gig workers need to report income?

Income earned from gig work is generally considered taxable income and should be tracked throughout the year.

What expenses might gig workers track?

Depending on the type of work performed, gig workers often track mileage, cell phone usage, supplies, equipment, and other business-related expenses.

Why should gig workers keep records year-round?

Staying organized throughout the year can make filing taxes easier and help reduce stress during tax season.

Musicians and Creative Professionals

Should musicians keep records of equipment purchases?

Keeping documentation for instruments, equipment, software, and other business purchases is often important for tax purposes.

Can musicians track travel and performance expenses?

Many creative professionals maintain records of travel, lodging, equipment, and other expenses connected to their professional activities.

Why is bookkeeping important for musicians?

Income often comes from multiple sources, making organized recordkeeping especially important.

Online Sellers

Should online sellers track inventory purchases?

Maintaining records of inventory, shipping costs, fees, and business expenses helps online sellers stay organized.

What financial records should online sellers keep?

Many sellers track sales reports, inventory purchases, marketplace fees, shipping expenses, and banking activity.

Why is bookkeeping important for ecommerce businesses?

Accurate records help business owners understand profitability and prepare for tax filing.
