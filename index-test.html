Own Your Taxes

Tax Education Made Simple

Practical tax guides for small business owners, contractors, freelancers, musicians, gig workers, and online sellers.

The goal is simple: help taxpayers understand taxes, recordkeeping, and common filing issues using plain English instead of technical jargon.

Why These Guides?

Taxes aren't the same for everyone. A musician faces different tax issues than a contractor or online seller. Each Own Your Taxes guide focuses on the deductions, filing requirements, recordkeeping, and tax topics most relevant to a specific type of taxpayer.
