Online Sellers Edition

The Online Sellers Edition is designed for ecommerce entrepreneurs, marketplace sellers, and individuals who generate income through online sales platforms.

Whether you're selling full-time or operating a side business, this guide provides practical tax education focused on organization, recordkeeping, and understanding common tax responsibilities associated with online sales.

Who This Book Is For

  • Marketplace sellers
  • Amazon sellers
  • eBay sellers

Topics Covered

  • Understanding online sales income
  • Recordkeeping and organization
  • Inventory basics
  • Shipping and fulfillment expenses
  • Platform fees and selling costs
  • Business expense tracking
  • Estimated tax considerations
  • Preparing for tax season

Why This Edition Matters

Many online sellers begin as hobbyists or part-time entrepreneurs and quickly discover that growing sales can create additional tax and recordkeeping responsibilities. Having a simple system for tracking income and expenses can make tax season much less stressful.

The Online Sellers Edition was created to help ecommerce entrepreneurs better understand the financial side of running an online business and build confidence throughout the year.
