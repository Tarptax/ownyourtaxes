Small Business Edition

The Small Business Edition is designed for entrepreneurs, sole proprietors, freelancers, and small business owners who want to better understand their tax responsibilities and opportunities.

Written in plain English, this guide focuses on practical tax education and recordkeeping strategies that can help business owners stay organized and make informed financial decisions throughout the year.

  • New business owners
  • Sole proprietors
  • LLC owners
  • Side-hustle entrepreneurs
  • Independent professionals

Topics Covered

  • Business income basics
  • Recordkeeping and organization
  • Tracking expenses
  • Common deductions
  • Estimated tax payments
  • Business tax forms
  • Financial organization strategies
  • Tax planning fundamentals

Why Tax Education Matters

Many small business owners start their businesses because they are passionate about their products or services—not taxes. Understanding basic tax concepts can help business owners stay organized, make informed decisions, and approach tax season with greater confidence.

The goal of the Own Your Taxes series is to make tax education more approachable through straightforward explanations and practical examples.
