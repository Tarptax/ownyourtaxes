Quarterly Tax Calendar

Use this calendar to stay organized throughout the year and keep important tax-related deadlines on your radar.

This calendar is intended as a planning tool and should not replace official IRS guidance regarding filing and payment deadlines.

January

  • □ Review prior-year income and expenses.
  • □ Organize receipts and financial records.
  • □ Prepare for upcoming tax season.
  • □ Verify estimated tax payment records.

February

  • □ Collect tax forms and statements.
  • □ Review bookkeeping records.
  • □ Confirm income records are complete.
  • □ Organize supporting documentation.

March

  • □ Review tax return preparation progress.
  • □ Locate any missing records.
  • □ Review mileage logs.
  • □ Organize expense documentation.

April

Quarterly Planning Month

  • □ Review first-quarter income.
  • □ Review first-quarter expenses.
  • □ Update bookkeeping records.
  • □ Review cash flow and tax planning.

May

  • □ Reconcile business accounts.
  • □ Organize digital records.
  • □ Review business goals.
  • □ Catch up on any missing bookkeeping.

June

Mid-Year Review

  • □ Review year-to-date income.
  • □ Review year-to-date expenses.
  • □ Verify mileage records.
  • □ Update tax planning estimates.

July

  • □ Organize receipts and invoices.
  • □ Review financial reports.
  • □ Verify account reconciliations.
  • □ Continue regular recordkeeping.

August

  • □ Review business performance.
  • □ Check for missing records.
  • □ Update income tracking systems.
  • □ Review upcoming year-end plans.

September

Third-Quarter Review

  • □ Review income and expenses.
  • □ Update mileage logs.
  • □ Organize documentation.
  • □ Begin thinking about year-end planning.

October

  • □ Review bookkeeping accuracy.
  • □ Organize digital files.
  • □ Review business goals.
  • □ Prepare for year-end recordkeeping.

November

  • □ Review income trends.
  • □ Organize expense records.
  • □ Review estimated tax planning.
  • □ Identify recordkeeping improvements.

December

Year-End Planning Month

  • □ Complete year-end record review.
  • □ Organize receipts and documentation.
  • □ Verify income records.
  • □ Review mileage logs.
  • □ Prepare for the upcoming tax season.

Monthly Checklist

  • □ Track income.
  • □ Record expenses.
  • □ Save receipts.
  • □ Update mileage records.
  • □ Reconcile bank accounts.
  • □ Review financial activity.

Tip: Tax season is easier when organization becomes a year-round habit. Small monthly reviews can prevent major headaches later.
