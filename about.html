Why I Created Own Your Taxes

Own Your Taxes was created with a simple mission: make tax education accessible to everyday Americans.

Tax information is often written for professionals instead of the people who actually need it. Small business owners, contractors, musicians, online sellers, and gig workers deserve practical guidance written in plain English.

The Own Your Taxes series helps readers understand tax concepts, identify opportunities, avoid common mistakes, and make more confident financial decisions throughout the year.
